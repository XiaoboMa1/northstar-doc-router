Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement is entered into between Acme Holdings LLC and Beta Integrations Inc., effective as of 2026-05-01, and shall remain in force for a term of twenty-four consecutive months unless terminated earlier pursuant to Section 8. The parties agree to the obligations set forth in Schedule A, including the deliverables, the service level commitments, and the escalation procedures described therein. Acme Holdings LLC shall provide integration engineering, platform operations, and second-tier technical support services at the rates detailed in Schedule B. Beta Integrations Inc. shall provide timely access to its test environments, shall make available the designated subject matter experts on each weekly sync, and shall pay undisputed billing statements on net-thirty terms. Either party may terminate this agreement upon sixty days written notice in the event of a material breach that remains uncured after a thirty day remediation window. Non-payment of an undisputed billing statement beyond ninety days shall constitute a material breach. No refund applies to pre-paid annual service fees under any termination initiated by Beta Integrations Inc. The renewal clause provides that this agreement will auto-renew for successive twelve month periods unless either party delivers a non-renewal notice at least sixty days prior to the end of the then-current term. Confidential information exchanged under this agreement shall be treated in accordance with the mutual non-disclosure terms attached as Schedule C. The governing law is the State of Delaware.
